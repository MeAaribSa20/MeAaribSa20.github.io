Recipe #1
Mom's Salsa

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Green Tomato 7
Roma Tomato 3
Cerano Chili 3-5
Garlic Clove 1/2
Salt Pinch
Onion Your Choice
Cilantro Your Choice
  1. Add water into a saucepan
  2. Add all the tomatos, cerano chilli, salt, and garlic clove, let boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Then, blend for a few minutes.
  4. Chop your cilantro and onion and salt.
  5. Finally, mix it all together!

Recipe #3
Pakistani Tea

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Boiling Water 1 1/2 cups
Tea Leaves 1 tsp
Milk 1/2 cup
Sugar Your Choice
  1. Turn on your Stove.
  2. Boil water.
  3. Add tea leaves, wait until color comes out.
  4. Add Sugar.
  5. Add milk.
  6. Boil it till' milk is mixed thoroughly.
  7. Pour into a cup through a sieve.
  8. Enjoy!
